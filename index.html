    Frequently asked questions

    What is a Guest Post Repository?
    A guest post repository is a database of websites accepting guest posts, organized by categories and metrics like Domain Authority.
    How to Identify a High-Quality Guest Post Site?
    Look for high Domain Authority, organic traffic, clear submission guidelines, and active audience engagement.
    Are Guest Posts Free or Paid?
    Some sites offer free posting opportunities, while others charge for premium placements or featured content.
    Do Guest Posts Improve SEO?
    Yes, guest posts can improve SEO by providing backlinks, increasing site authority, and driving referral traffic.
    How Often Should I Post on Guest Sites?
    Consistency matters—aim for 1–2 quality posts per month on relevant, authoritative sites to maintain impact.
